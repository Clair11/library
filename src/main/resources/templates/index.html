National Library of China

The national library of China is divided into the total museum, the north pavilion and the ancient book museum, with 3119 million books, among which there are more than 2 million copies of ancient books.In 2008, the building area of China's national library was 280,000 square meters. It is the largest library in Asia and the third largest in the world.

Library of the British Museum

The British library (also translated as the British library, the national library of England) is one of the largest academic libraries in the world. The British library act of 1972 was established on 1 July 1973.The library is located in London and west Yorkshire. It is composed of the former British museum library, the national central library, the national library of science and technology library and the national bibliographic publishing house.
